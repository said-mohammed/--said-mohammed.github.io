Innovative climate practices

Smart practices

We are a regional organization that identifies and
develops appropriate innovative climate smart practices
and builds sustainable and cost effective operational
solutions for implementation

At the 5th Global YES Rework the World Summit 2010 in Sweden, over 150 replicable projects were showcased in the themes of People, Water, Energy, Cities and Land highlighting the great potential benefits from sharing and scaling best practices. The Green Teams approach was introduced to understand how to bring together different people and projects to collaborate for a shared purpose, to learn and to scale solutions.

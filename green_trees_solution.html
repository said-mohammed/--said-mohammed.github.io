Green Trees Solutions

Our primary goal for the Green Trees Solutions is to inspire the planting of at least 10 Million indigenous, endemic and fruit trees across Kenya by 2033.

Tree Nurseries will be established in 20 Counties across the country. Saplings of carefully selected tree species will be raised in the nurseries. The Green Teams in schools will be our primary locations where students will be taught on the importance of growing trees and increasing the forest cover across the country. It is expected the skills and knowledge learned in the Green Clubs in schools will then be transferred to the homes where the students come from thus inspiring their parents and other community members to participate in the mass tree planting sessions during the rainy seasons.

We shall partner with County Governments to provide the spaces for the tree nurseries and schools for the establishment of Green Clubs where the skills and skills transfer will take place in fun filled activities involving the learners. Kenya's Environment is in a balance. As populations continue to double every twenty years, the demand for forest resources will double. The forest cover stands at less than 7% with most of it being exotic species (Plantation forest).

Did You Know?

Kenya has over 7000 taxa and over 1200 genus yet Kenya's plant only three to four types of trees which are exotic and have negative effect to infrastructure, people and environment.
